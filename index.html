About Me

I am a passionate engineer and researcher specializing in Artificial Intelligence for real world dynamical systems such as biomedical systems and computational agriculture.

With a First Class Honours degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Peradeniya (GPA: 3.80/4.0), I have developed expertise in machine learning, embedded systems, and signal processing. My research has been recognized through multiple publications and awards at national and international levels.

I hold a Diploma in International Relations from Bandaranaike Centre for International Studies (Merit) and have completed the Operational level of Chartered Institute of Management Accountants.

Beyond technical pursuits, I am an accomplished public speaker with a TEDx talk and multiple national speaking championships. I believe in the power of communication to bridge the gap between complex technology and societal impact.
